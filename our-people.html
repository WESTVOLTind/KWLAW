Meet Our Team

At Karanja & Wanjiru Advocates, our strength lies in the collective expertise and dedication of our legal professionals. Our team comprises highly skilled individuals with extensive experience across various fields of law. Led by the Managing Partner and supported by a network of strategic consultants, we provide tailored, results-driven legal solutions that cater to the unique needs of our clients.

Through strategic collaborations, we leverage the combined knowledge, skills, and experience of our consultants to deliver comprehensive legal services across corporate and commercial law, real estate, dispute resolution, and emerging sectors such as fintech and technology law. This collaborative approach ensures that our clients benefit from multidisciplinary legal support while maintaining the personalized attention of a boutique firm.
