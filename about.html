Who We Are - Karanja & Wanjiru Advocates

Established in September 2015, Karanja & Wanjiru Advocates is a dynamic, innovative, and internationally connected law firm committed to providing top-tier legal solutions in an increasingly globalized world. Founded by British-Kenyan legal professionals with extensive experience in Kenya’s top law firms and legal training from the UK, our firm bridges the gap between Kenyan and international legal systems, offering specialized support to businesses, individuals, and diaspora clients navigating cross-border legal matters.

With deep roots in Kenya and strong ties to the UK and the broader global market, we understand the legal, commercial, and personal challenges faced by individuals and businesses operating across different jurisdictions. Our clients—both locally and in the diaspora—trust us not only for our legal expertise but also for our cultural awareness, business insight, and ability to provide tailored legal solutions that transcend borders.

At Karanja & Wanjiru Advocates, we don’t just practice law—we create connections, facilitate opportunities, and provide strategic legal guidance in a digitally evolving world. By leveraging cutting-edge technology, a modern digital presence, and international networks, we ensure that our clients receive efficient, forward-thinking legal support—wherever they are in the world.

Whether you’re a diaspora investor, an international business, or a local client looking for world-class legal representation, Karanja & Wanjiru Advocates is your trusted partner—bridging legal, business, and regulatory gaps across Kenya, the UK, and beyond.

Our firm prides itself on being bold, innovative, and digitally connected. We embrace modern technology, legal-tech solutions, and thought leadership to provide accessible, efficient, and transformative legal services. We are not just legal practitioners—we are strategic advisors and partners in your success.
