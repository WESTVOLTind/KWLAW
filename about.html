Who We Are - Karanja & Wanjiru Advocates

Karanja & Wanjiru Advocates is a leading law firm in Kenya, providing comprehensive legal services to individuals, businesses, and international clients.

With a strong presence in Kenya and a growing footprint in the diaspora, we specialize in offering tailored legal solutions that address the unique needs of our diverse clientele. Our firm is founded on professionalism, confidentiality, and client-centered advocacy, ensuring that every legal matter is handled with precision and excellence.

Our Mission

To offer exceptional legal services that uphold justice, fairness, and professionalism, ensuring that our clients receive the best legal representation in Kenya and beyond.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading law firm in Kenya and internationally, known for top-tier legal services, innovative solutions, and unwavering dedication to client success.

Our Core Values

Integrity, Professionalism, Client-Centric Approach, Excellence, Confidentiality.

Why Choose Karanja and Wanjiru Advocates?

  • Experienced Legal Team: Our advocates bring years of expertise in various legal fields, ensuring top-notch legal representation.
  • Wide Range of Legal Services: From corporate law, property transactions, family law, litigation, commercial law, immigration law, and legal advisory, we cover all your legal needs.
  • Local and International Reach: Whether you're in Kenya or abroad, we provide seamless legal assistance tailored to your jurisdiction.
  • Proven Track Record: We have successfully handled complex legal cases, corporate transactions, and dispute resolutions, making us a trusted partner in the legal industry.
  • Technology-Driven Approach: Our firm leverages modern legal technology to enhance efficiency, research, and case management.

Our Commitment to the Diaspora

Karanja & Wanjiru Advocates recognizes the unique legal challenges faced by Kenyans living abroad. We specialize in: Real estate transactions in Kenya for diaspora clients. Immigration and visa applications. Business registration and investment advisory for expatriates. Legal representation in family law matters, inheritance, and succession planning. We simplify complex legal processes, ensuring that diaspora clients receive timely and reliable legal solutions while residing overseas.
