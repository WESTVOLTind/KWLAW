Portfolio

Mukami Kuria is a highly experienced legal practitioner specializing in real estate, conveyancing, and banking & financial services law. She holds an LL.B from the University of Nairobi and an LL.M in Corporate Governance and Financial Regulation from Warwick University. She was admitted to the Kenyan Bar in 2012, giving her over 12 years of post-admission legal experience.

Mukami has built a distinguished career working in some of Kenya’s leading law firms, focusing on property law, banking transactions, and complex conveyancing matters. She has played a pivotal role in structuring, negotiating, and securing financing for some of Kenya’s most notable real estate developments, representing both local and international investors, financial institutions, and private developers.

Her deep expertise in banking and financial law has also seen her advise major commercial banks, mortgage lenders, and financial service providers on regulatory compliance, security documentation, and corporate lending transactions.
